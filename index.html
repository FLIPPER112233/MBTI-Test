About MBTI

What is MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) categorizes individuals into 16 distinct personality types based on four dichotomies.

E vs I

Extraversion vs Introversion

S vs N

Sensing vs Intuition

T vs F

Thinking vs Feeling

J vs P

Judging vs Perceiving

How This App Works

  • Students take a 20-question personality test
  • Results determine their MBTI type
  • Teachers can create unique seating charts based on MBTI types built on an algorithim by Buddy Up!
